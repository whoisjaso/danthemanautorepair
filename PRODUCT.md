# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Drivers in Humble, North Houston, and nearby communities who need practical auto repair help quickly. They may be dealing with warning lights, brake noise, overheating, no-start issues, A/C problems, tire trouble, or routine maintenance and need a local shop that feels direct, capable, and easy to contact.

## Product Purpose

Dan The Man Auto Repair's website is a single-page lead-generation surface. It should make the shop's services, location, phone number, and service request path immediately clear, then drive visitors to call, get directions, or book service.

## Brand Personality

Fast, mechanical, trustworthy. The brand should feel like a performance garage: black carbon texture, red neon urgency, chrome type, mechanic confidence, and local service clarity.

## Anti-references

Do not make this look like a generic mechanic template, a flat white service-business page, a pastel local-business site, a blue/red auto franchise theme, or a cluttered image flyer squeezed into a webpage.

## Design Principles

1. Call first: every major viewport must make the phone number or booking action obvious.
2. Build the flyer as HTML: services, menus, forms, and contact details must be readable, responsive, and editable.
3. Performance garage, not stock garage: use red neon, carbon texture, metallic panels, wrench/engine/car visuals, and controlled motion.
4. Local trust over exaggerated claims: use clear, practical copy and editable review placeholders.
5. Mobile is the sales floor: tap targets, sticky actions, problem selection, and the form must work cleanly on phones.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text, controls, form labels, focus states, and placeholders. Support reduced motion, keyboard navigation, readable mobile service rows, and 44px minimum touch targets for primary mobile actions.
